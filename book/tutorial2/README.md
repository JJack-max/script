## Prerequisites

Before starting this tutorial series, you should have:
- Understanding of basic computer science concepts
- Familiarity with distributed systems principles
- Basic knowledge of cryptography
- Interest in blockchain technology fundamentals

## Approach

This series takes a theoretical approach to understanding Solana, with:
- Mathematical explanations of core concepts
- Comparisons with traditional distributed systems
- Analysis of trade-offs and design decisions
- Discussion of security assumptions and guarantees
