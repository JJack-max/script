## Content Focus

This tutorial series differs from the practical series in several key ways:

### Theoretical vs Practical
- **Practical Series**: "How to build on Solana"
- **Principles Series**: "Why Solana works the way it does"

### Target Audience
- **Practical Series**: Developers and builders
- **Principles Series**: Researchers, architects, and theorists

### Content Depth
- **Practical Series**: Hands-on coding and implementation
- **Principles Series**: Mathematical foundations and theoretical analysis

### Learning Outcomes
- **Practical Series**: Skills to build Solana applications
- **Principles Series**: Deep understanding of Solana's architecture
